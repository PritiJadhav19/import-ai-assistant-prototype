HSN basics:
HSN is the Harmonized System of Nomenclature used to classify goods for customs and GST.

Common examples:
LED lamps and lighting fittings often fall under HSN 9405 (depends on product type).
Toys generally fall under HSN 9503.
Electric motors are commonly under HSN 8501.
Solar cells/modules are commonly under HSN 8541.

Compliance hints (high-level):
Wireless devices may require WPC/ETA compliance.
Food items may require FSSAI compliance.
Certain product categories may require BIS certification (e.g., toys/electronics in some cases).

Documents commonly required:
Commercial Invoice, Packing List, Bill of Lading/AWB, Certificate of Origin, Insurance (if applicable), Technical datasheet/specification, Importer IEC details.

Verification steps:
Check CBIC Customs Tariff for chapter notes and duty rates.
Check DGFT/ITC(HS) for restrictions or licensing requirements.
Use ICEGATE references for filing and clearance process.
