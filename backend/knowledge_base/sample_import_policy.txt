Vilican Internal Import Classification Guide (2026 Edition)

Section 1: Toys Classification
Most plastic toys for children under age 14 are classified under HSN 9503.
These may require BIS compliance certification under Indian safety standards.
If product includes electronics or battery components, additional testing may be required.

Section 2: LED Lighting Products
LED decorative lighting fittings are typically classified under HSN 9405.
Duty rate may vary depending on whether it is a finished product or component.
Verify applicable tariff heading in CBIC Customs Tariff Schedule.

Section 3: Solar Equipment
Solar photovoltaic modules and cells generally fall under HSN 8541.
Certain solar equipment may be eligible for concessional duty under renewable energy policy.
Check latest CBIC notifications for updated duty structure.

Section 4: Wireless Products
Products containing WiFi, Bluetooth, or RF modules require WPC/ETA approval before import.
Model number and RF frequency details must be declared.

Section 5: Required Documents
All imports require:
- Commercial Invoice
- Packing List
- Bill of Lading / Airway Bill
- Certificate of Origin
- IEC (Importer Exporter Code)
Additional compliance certificates may be required depending on product category.
